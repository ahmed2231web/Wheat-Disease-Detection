About AgroConnect

AgroConnect is an innovative platform designed to help farmers identify and manage wheat diseases using artificial intelligence and expert advice.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with accessible technology that helps them identify plant diseases early, reduce crop losses, and implement effective treatment strategies.

By combining advanced AI image recognition with expert agricultural knowledge, we provide a comprehensive solution for wheat disease management that is accessible to farmers everywhere.

We believe that technology should serve agriculture, making farming more sustainable, productive, and resilient in the face of challenges like climate change and emerging plant diseases.

How AgroConnect Works

1. Upload a Photo

Take a clear photo of your wheat plant showing any signs of disease or abnormality.

2. AI Analysis

Our advanced AI model analyzes the image to identify the disease with high accuracy.

3. Receive Diagnosis

Get detailed information about the detected disease, including symptoms and confidence level.

4. Chat with AI Assistant

Ask questions about the disease, treatment options, and prevention strategies to our AI-powered assistant.

Common Wheat Diseases

Leaf Spot

Appears as brown spots with yellow halos on wheat leaves, affecting photosynthesis and yield.

Crown Rot

A fungal disease that affects the crown and roots, causing yellowing and wilting of plants.

Healthy Wheat

Healthy wheat plants have vibrant green leaves without spots, lesions, or discoloration.
